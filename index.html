About Me

A Story of Art & Sound

I'm Isabeve Korkama — a senior student from the vibrant land of Kazakhstan, living at the intersection of music and visual art.

My journey began with a sketchpad and a melody humming in my head. Over the years, I've discovered that both art forms speak the same language — they make people feel something real. Whether I'm covering a song that moves me or drawing a portrait that captures emotion, my goal is always the same: to create something that resonates.

As a passionate singer, I love interpreting cover songs and weaving my own personality into every note. As a sketch artist, I find stillness and depth in pencil strokes and shading. Together, these two worlds form the complete expression of who I am.

— Isabeve Korkama
